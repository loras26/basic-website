Historical Places

Hector Pieterson Memorial

The museum is two blocks away from where Hector Pieterson was shot and killed 16 June 1976. The museum features films, newspapers, personal accounts and photographs, the most famous being the iconic photo by Sam Nzima.

Apartheid Museum

Apartheid museum illustrates the rise and fall of apartheid. The exhibits are from film footages, photographs, text panels and artifacts illustrating the events and human stories that are part of the epic saga, known as apartheid.

Museum Africa

Museum Africa is a journey back into the glory years of the Africa continent’s past, when the first civilisations thrived. Its collection and research focuses on indigenous African cultures, history and archaeology, and linguistics collection.
